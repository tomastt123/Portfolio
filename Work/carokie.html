Car Accessories online shop - Carokie

The Carokie Website is an e-commerce platform designed for selling car accessories and parts. Developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Shopify, this site offers a modern, streamlined shopping experience for car enthusiasts and professionals. The website integrates seamlessly with Shopify's e-commerce functionalities, providing features like product listings, shopping cart, and secure payment processing. With a responsive design, users can easily browse products, check availability, and place orders from any device, ensuring a smooth and efficient shopping experience.

Key features include:

  • Fully integrated e-commerce functionality with Shopify
  • Responsive design for easy navigation across all devices
  • Product filtering and search functionality for quick browsing
  • Secure checkout and payment gateway integration
