Hotel Management Application

This project involves the development of a comprehensive Hotel Management System aimed at automating and optimizing various hotel operations, such as room bookings, customer check-ins and check-outs, and managing reservations. Built using C++ and MySQL, the system enhances efficiency by streamlining complex workflows and ensuring accurate data management. It offers an intuitive user interface for hotel staff to easily manage guest information, track room availability, and generate billing reports, making day-to-day hotel operations faster and more reliable.

Key features include:

  • Automated room reservation and booking system
  • Efficient customer data management
  • Real-time tracking of room availability
  • Generation of billing and financial reports
